Everstorm Music Share

About Us

Everstorm Music Share is a 'proof of concept' music hosting app for local musicians.

If this project were to grow beyond the scope of this capstone, we would like to add other local artist, bios, and music to share

With the DynamoDB already connected to this app, we could add location and genre based search queries to allow users to find local music in their area.

Our Music

allpersonsficctitious started out as a small studio project between Grey and Scott.

Grey had envisioned music to go along with some of his favorite fantasy novels and requested help from his long time friend to make that dream come true.

Everstorm is a Parshendi war song, based in the world of Brandon Sandersons' Stormlight Archive.

Lyrics and vocals are by Grey, while Scott produced, recorded and mixed everything else.


Oceus (Only Child Syndrome) is purely the creation of Scott, writting, composing and recording all instruments, lyrics and vocals.
